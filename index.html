How to Use hornhüd

Tagging an Address

Zoom in and tap any building outline on the map. If it's already tagged, you'll see its details. If it's new, an edit form opens with the address auto-filled from GPS.

Reading the Map

Buildings are color-coded: blue for donkey, red for elephant. Favorites get a gold outline. Disposition shows as a small emoji (😊 or 😠) on the building.

Editing a Record

From the detail view, tap Edit. Change disposition, affiliation, contacts, dogs, notes, or add your own custom tags.

Searching & Filtering

Use the search bar to find addresses or contact names. Tap 🏷️ to filter by disposition, affiliation, or stars. Tap Clear to reset.

Stars

Tap the star on any address to mark it. Stars are personal — each user has their own — but visible to everyone.

Interactions

Tap + Log in the detail view to record when you connected with someone. Shows who logged it and when.

Importing & Exporting

1
Open List from the bottom bar.
2
Export: Tap 📤 to download a JSON backup.
3
Import: Tap 📥 and select a JSON file.

Saving to Contacts

Tap 📇 vCard in the detail view to create a contact card your iPhone can add to Contacts.
