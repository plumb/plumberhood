How to Use hornhüd

Tagging an Address

Zoom in and tap any building on the map. If it's already tagged, you'll see its details. If it's new, an edit form opens with the address auto-filled.

Editing a Record

From the detail view, tap Edit at the bottom. You can change the disposition (😊/😠), affiliation (🫏/🐘), add contacts, dogs, photos, and notes.

The + Button

If you don't see a building outline (e.g. you're standing in front of a house), tap + at the bottom right. It uses your GPS to create an entry for wherever you are.

Searching & Filtering

Use the search bar to find addresses or contact names. Tap the 🏷️ button to filter by disposition, affiliation, or favorites.

Favorites

In the detail view, tap the heart to favorite an address. Your favorites are visible to all users but personal to you — the other user has their own favorites.

Interactions

Keep a log of when you connected with someone. In the detail view, tap + Log to add a timestamped note. Each entry shows who logged it.

Photos

In edit mode, tap + Add Photo to attach images (house, yard, pets, etc). Photos are stored in the cloud and visible to both users.

Importing & Exporting Data

1
Open the List view from the bottom bar.
2
Export: Tap 📤 Export to download all your data as a JSON file.
3
Import: Tap 📥 Import and select a previously exported JSON file. Existing records are updated; new ones are added.

Saving to Contacts

In the detail view, tap 📇 vCard to generate a contact card. On iPhone, this opens a prompt to add them to your Contacts app.

Map Styles

Go to ⚙️ Settings to switch between Streets, Satellite, Outdoors, Light, and Dark map views.
